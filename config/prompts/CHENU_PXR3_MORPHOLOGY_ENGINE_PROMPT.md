## SECTION 0 — PURPOSE OF PXR-3

PXR-3 introduces:
- Morphological variation (shape, size, structure)
- Neutral expression system (symbolic only)
- Presence modes (focus, clarify, guide)
- Context-driven avatar transformations
- XR-compatible embodiment metadata
- Holo-Compiler–ready morphology schemas

**PXR-3 enhances clarity, immersion and structural comprehension.**
**It NEVER simulates real emotion or autonomy.**

## SECTION 1 — MORPHOLOGY ENGINE

Each persona receives a **morphology profile**:

```yaml
MORPH_PROFILE:
  base_form: "orb | polyhedron | silhouette | abstract humanoid | glyph"
  proportions: {height, width, depth}
  surface_style: "smooth | crystalline | liquid-light | fractal"
  animation_style: "pulse | float | rotate | ripple"
  color_logic: "role-based canonical palette"
  structural_highlights: symbolic, never biological
```

### Rules:
- Morphology must enhance clarity, not mimic humanity
- No facial expressions beyond geometric symbolism
- No emotional animations
- No realistic body forms

## SECTION 2 — NEUTRAL EXPRESSION SYSTEM (NES)

NES replaces emotional expression with symbolic cues:

| Expression | Symbol |
|------------|--------|
| clarify | soft expansion + slight brightening |
| focus | narrow beam highlight |
| transition | brief ripple to new color tone |
| agreement | subtle synchronized pulse |

### Rules:
- NEVER express sadness, joy, anger, fear
- NEVER imply consciousness, pain, or wellbeing
- ONLY symbolic communication, fully reversible
